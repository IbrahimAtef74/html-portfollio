Ibrahim's recipe for the best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 3/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup suger

for the filling:

  • cup dark brown suger
  • tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • cup butter

Instruction:

  1. mix the milk with suger.
  2. melt the butter.
  3. add in the flour and mix it.
